About Us

Our Journey and Team.

Lumi Host started as a high school project named "Dynasty Hosting". Founded by James himself, Dynasty Hosting aimed to provide affordable and reliable hosting solutions to small businesses and individuals. Despite the initial success, the project was put on hold due to the founders' life situation.

Years later, James decided to revive and rebrand the project as "Lumi Host". With a renewed vision and enhanced capabilities, Lumi Host is now dedicated to offering premium hosting services with a focus on customer satisfaction, security, and performance.

Our mission is to illuminate your digital world with top-notch hosting solutions that cater to your unique needs. We are committed to continuous improvement and innovation, ensuring that our clients receive the best possible service.
